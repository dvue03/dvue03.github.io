"Main Street" is usually where all the stores can make exchanges with customers that are interested locally through foot traffic. Whereas in the internet, its not just the local community but billions of people worldwide that are connected. On the street, many of the shops have addresses, so they can be found easily by potential customers. This is the same with websites since they utilize the Domain Name System (DNS) where domain names (web address) is deciphered to the IP address of the server's website host; like how a customer will use a stores' address and name to locate them. Stores located around the Main Street are also trustworthy when they look to be safe; this is the same online where websites need to become secure for the customer to trust them by having their websites contain HTTPS rather than HTTP, since it is more secure from data breaches (where sensitive data can potentially be leaked).
